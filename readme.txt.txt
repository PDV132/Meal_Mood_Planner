# Mood-Based Meal Planner

This project is a mood-based meal planner that suggests meals based on your current mood. You can input your mood via text or by recording your voice.

## Features

- **Mood Detection**: Detects your mood from text input.
- **Meal Suggestions**: Recommends meals from a predefined list based on your mood.
- **Voice-to-Text**: Transcribes your voice to text for mood analysis.
- **Meal Logging**: Logs your meal suggestions for future reference.
